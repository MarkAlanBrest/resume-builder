PROGRAM INFORMATION — BUILDING TECHNOLOGY
Main Campus — New Castle, PA

Program Name
Building Technology — Main Campus (New Castle, PA)
Credential Earned
Associate in Specialized Technology

Program Overview (Resume-Safe)
The Building Technology program prepares students for entry-level positions in residential and light commercial construction. Training emphasizes carpentry fundamentals, framing, interior and exterior construction, and basic project concepts through classroom instruction and hands-on lab experience.

Primary Technical Training Areas
Students receive instruction and hands-on exposure in:
* Residential and light commercial framing
* Roofing systems
* Window and door installation
* Interior construction and finishing
* Exterior construction methods
* Blueprint interpretation and basic building codes
* Introductory project planning concepts

Hands-On & Lab Experience
As part of the program, students typically participate in lab-based activities involving:
* Framing walls, floors, and basic roof structures
* Measuring, layout, and material preparation
* Installation of drywall, trim, and interior finishes
* Window and door installation
* Use of hand and power tools on simulated jobsite projects
* Applying jobsite safety practices
Include only experiences confirmed by the student.
