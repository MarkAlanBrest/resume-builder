PROGRAM INFORMATION — COMMERCIAL TRUCK DRIVING (CLASS A CDL)
Satellite Campus — Pulaski, PA

Program Name
Commercial Truck Driving (Class A CDL) — Satellite Campus (Pulaski, PA)
Credential Earned
CDL Class A (only if earned)

Program Overview (Resume-Safe)
The Commercial Truck Driving (Class A CDL) program prepares students for entry-level commercial driving careers through classroom instruction and supervised behind-the-wheel training. Training emphasizes safety, regulatory compliance, vehicle operation, and real-world driving fundamentals.

Primary Training Areas
Students receive instruction and supervised practice in:
* Department of Transportation (DOT) regulations
* Pre-trip and post-trip vehicle inspections
* Backing maneuvers
* On-road driving procedures
* Trip planning and route documentation

Hands-On & Driving Experience
As part of the program, students typically participate in training activities involving:
* Conducting full pre-trip and post-trip inspections
* Practicing backing maneuvers (straight-line, offset, alley dock)
* Supervised road driving in tractor-trailer equipment
* Trip planning and route documentation exercises
* Application of safety procedures and compliance standards
Include only experiences confirmed by the student.

Commercial Driving Skills Developed
Applicable resume skills may include:
* Pre-trip and post-trip inspections
* Backing maneuvers
* Road driving under supervision
* Trip planning and routing
* Logbook and documentation management
* DOT safety and compliance practices
* Equipment awareness and basic vehicle operation

Tools & Equipment Exposure
Students may gain experience using:
* Tractor-trailer equipment
* Vehicle inspection tools
* Logbook systems (electronic or paper, as applicable)
Include tools only if used by the student.
