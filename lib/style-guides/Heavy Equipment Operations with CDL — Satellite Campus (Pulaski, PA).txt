PROGRAM INFORMATION — HEAVY EQUIPMENT OPERATIONS WITH CDL
Satellite Campus — Pulaski, PA

Program Name
Heavy Equipment Operations with CDL — Satellite Campus (Pulaski, PA)
Credential Earned
Commercial Driver’s License (CDL) (only if earned)
Heavy Equipment Operations Training

Program Overview (Resume-Safe)
The Heavy Equipment Operations with CDL program prepares students for entry-level positions operating heavy equipment and commercial vehicles. Training emphasizes safe equipment operation, jobsite awareness, inspections, basic maintenance concepts, and CDL-related driving fundamentals through hands-on instruction.

Primary Training Areas
Students receive instruction and supervised practice in:
* Heavy equipment operation fundamentals
* CDL driving principles and vehicle operation
* Pre-operation and post-operation equipment inspections
* Jobsite safety procedures
* Equipment transportation basics
* Basic operational maintenance awareness

Hands-On & Equipment Experience
As part of the program, students typically participate in training activities involving:
* Operating heavy equipment under instructor supervision
* Performing daily equipment inspection procedures
* Practicing safe equipment operation techniques
* Conducting CDL-related driving and transportation exercises
* Applying jobsite safety rules and communication practices
Include only experiences confirmed by the student.
