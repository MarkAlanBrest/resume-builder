Program Overview (Resume-Safe)
The Refrigeration & Climate Control program prepares students for entry-level roles working with refrigeration and climate control systems. Training emphasizes system installation, service procedures, diagnostics, electrical troubleshooting, preventive maintenance, and safety practices through hands-on lab experience.

Primary Technical Training Areas
Students receive instruction and hands-on exposure in:
* Refrigeration systems
* Climate control systems
* Electrical components and controls
* System diagnostics and performance evaluation
* Preventive maintenance procedures
* Safety and environmental practices

Hands-On & Lab Experience
As part of the program, students typically participate in lab-based activities involving:
* Installing and servicing refrigeration and climate control systems
* Diagnosing system performance issues
* Performing electrical troubleshooting on climate control equipment
* Conducting system inspections and preventive maintenance tasks
* Applying safety and environmental standards
Include only experiences confirmed by the student.
